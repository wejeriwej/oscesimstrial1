About Us





My name is Zak, and I am a Foundation Year 2 doctor currently working in Leicester. I completed my medical degree at Hull York Medical School, where I developed a strong passion for both clinical practice and medical education.


In my free time, I enjoy learning how to code, as I believe that technology has the potential to enhance medical education and improve accessibility to high-quality learning resources. This passion led me to create OSCE Sims, a platform designed to support medical students in preparing for their Objective Structured Clinical Examinations (OSCEs).


During my own training, I noticed a significant gap in resources available for independent OSCE practice. Many students struggle to find high-quality, structured materials, and practicing effectively often requires having a study partner, which isnâ€™t always feasible. Recognizing this challenge, I wanted to develop a solution that would allow students to practice OSCEs independently, ensuring they can refine their clinical skills and build confidence regardless of their circumstances.


Through OSCE Sims, my goal is to provide an interactive and accessible tool that empowers medical students to excel in their exams and, ultimately, become more competent and confident clinicians.
